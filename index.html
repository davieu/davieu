About Me

Growing up I always had a strong interest in technology and electronics, but before I began my journey to become a software engineer my previous career path was ingrained in logistics and sales. My satisfaction was dwindling in my current field and I needed a new transition. A transition where I could face new challenges and problem solve my way through them. With a new goal in life to learn to code I decided to start with the basics of HTML and CSS. Slowly I was chipping away at code and moving up to harder and complicated problems. I was continuously learning new skills everyday and loved the challenges that programming entailed. By dedicating more time to code, it further cemented my passion for coding and validated that this is the career change that I have been searching for.
